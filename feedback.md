## How to Write Good Feedback

Focus on **feel**, not solutions. You don't need to know how to fix it — just describe what you experienced.

Good: *"When I get hit it doesn't feel like it hurt, the flash is too subtle"*
Not as useful: *"Make the hit effect bigger"*

Good: *"I kept getting cornered in the bottom left corner and couldn't escape"*
Not as useful: *"Fix the movement"*

Rate each session: 🔴 Not fun yet | 🟡 Getting there | 🟢 This feels good
